Architect

An architect envisions and conceptualizes the very essence of a building. They are the artistic minds behind the blueprints, weaving aesthetics and functionality into a cohesive design. Architects consider factors like space utilization, environmental impact, and user experience to create structures that not only stand tall but also cater to the needs of those who inhabit them
